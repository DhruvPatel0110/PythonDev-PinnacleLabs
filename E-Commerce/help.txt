=================================
CUSTOMER
=================================
- Register as Customer.
- Log in with your email and password.
- Browse tech products such as CPUs, GPUs, RAM, storage, monitors, and accessories.
- Add products to your cart.
- Place orders and track delivery updates from your dashboard.
- Log out when you finish using your account.

=================================
SELLER
=================================
- Register as Seller.
- Enter your store name during registration.
- Log in to open your seller dashboard.
- Add tech products and manage inventory.
- View orders from customers.
- Update product listings, stock, pricing, and discounts.

=================================
DELIVERY AGENT
=================================
- Register as Delivery Agent.
- Add your vehicle type, vehicle number, and driving license number.
- Log in to open your delivery dashboard.
- View assigned deliveries.
- Update delivery status as orders move forward.
- Track completed deliveries from your account.

=================================
LOGIN HELP
=================================
- Use the same email and password you used during registration.
- Choose Remember me if you want to stay signed in on this device.
- Use the Show button to check your password while typing.
- If login fails, check your email spelling and password.
